Achievements

2018 - 2019

CATC

Volleyball Trophy

Cadets from BIT NCC won volleyball trophy for the 3rd consecutive time in CATC held at Hindusthan Institute of Technology.

Award

AWARD OF EXCELLENCE

Lt. Dr. Kalil Rahiman M received the Award of excellence for the year 2018 from Commodore Vijesh K Garg, Deputy Director General- NCC Tamil Nadu, Pondicherry and Andaman Nicobar Directorate and Colonel Peter Celestine, Group Commander, NCC Group Head Quarters, Coimbatore during the felicitation function held at Nirmala College, Coimbatore on 10.08.2018

KCT

WARFARE

Over 30 Colleges and 30 Schools participated in 3rd Annual Meet. 600 cadets from Army Wing and Air Force Wing comprising Senior Wing, Senior Division and Junior Division rejoiced in this united meet. The BIT NCC performance were outstanding in all events which resulted as Overall Championship and won the Warfare 2k19 Trophy.

IGC

GEM CADET

Cdt Arasan E had gone through various level of selection camps, Camp I at Angel College of Technology, Coimbatore, Camp II at Hindustan Institute of Technology, Tirupur, Pre IGC at KPR Institute of Technology, Coimbatore and finally in Inter Group Competitions, he has represented Coimbatore group in IGC held at KPR Institute Of Technology, Coimbatore from 08.10.2018 to 17.10.2018.

SECUNDERABAD

AAC

CUO Kavin Mughil, CUO Meiyarasan, Sgt Nethaji and Cdt Nishant attended the Army Attachment Camp at 1st Bihar regiment Army Base, Secunderabad from 05.11.2018 to 21.11.2018. CUO Meiyarassan and Cdt Nishanth secured 2nd and 3rd places in the 2.5 km athletic event. CUO Kavin Mughil secured 1st place in the drill competition and he was the camp Senior for all cadets who were under the first Bihar Regiment. Sgt Nethaji participated in Volley Ball competition and won the trophy

NAGERCOIL

NIC

SCUO Gnanamoorthi and Sgt Mohanraj successfully completed the National Integration Camp from 09.11.2018 to 18.11.2018 at Nagercoil.SCUO Gnanamoorthi represented Coimbatore Group in that prestigious national camp who was selected for his courage and discipline. Sgt Mohanraj represented Coimbatore Group as Volleyball captain and won runner up trophy with his hard work and dedication.

PSG MEDICAL COLLEGE

FELICITATION CEREMONY

Bannari Amman Institute sponsored 200 memento of Chief Minister’s Banner Winners 2019 as a award of appreciation to encourage the hard work and dedication for RDC, TSC, VSC and IGC of 2018-2019. BIT NCC’s Cdt Arasan is one of them to receive the memento. Ceremony took place in PSG Medical College, Coimbatore on 27.02.2019.

KAMPTEE

OFFICERS TRAINING ACADEMY

Lt. Dr. KALIL RAHIMAN M, Associate NCC Officer has completed the NCC Refresher Course at Officer’s Training Academy, Kamptee, Nagpur from 01.03.2019 to 30.03.2019, which inculcate the skills out of Military Training Course for future promotion in the levels of Army rank.
