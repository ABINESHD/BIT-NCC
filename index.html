About US

BIT NCC

National Cadet Corps in Bannari Amman Institute of Technology commenced in the year of 1999. BIT has the prestigious Army wing of Senior Division NCC. Young soldiers will be seen trained extensively in parade ground of the college campus. Cadets show their excellent display of skill of marching at the Independence ,Republic days and sports days. They attend various camps being held in both inter and intra states.
